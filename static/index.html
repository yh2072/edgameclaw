How it works

Three Steps to Gamified Learning

No game design experience needed. Just paste your content and let AI do the rest.

1 📝

Paste Your Content

Add any learning material — textbooks, notes, research papers, or training manuals — in Markdown format.

2 🤖

AI Generates Games

Our AI pipeline analyzes your content and creates custom mini-games that match each concept's ideal learning mechanic.

3 🎮

Play & Learn

Each chapter becomes an interactive game with pixel art visuals, dialog, and scoring — playable instantly in your browser.

Features

🌍

8 Languages

Generate courses in English, Chinese, Japanese, Spanish, French, Korean, Arabic, and German.

🎨

24 Visual Themes
